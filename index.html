About

Young Researchers Workshop on Applied Mathematics is a one-day workshop in Kanazawa, Japan, bringing together graduate students, young researchers, and experts working on a wide range of topics in applied mathematics. The workshop provides a forum for presenting and discussing recent advances in computational science, mathematical modeling, and related fields. The event aims to foster academic exchange, encourage collaboration, and support the development of early-career researchers.
